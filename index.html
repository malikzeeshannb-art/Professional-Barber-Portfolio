About Me

I’m Malik Sufyan, a professional barber with 3+ years of hands-on experience. I started my journey in Chakwal, where I spent 2 years building a strong foundation in classic and modern barbering while understanding client needs and consistency.

I then worked for 1 year in Lahore near Emporium Mall, where I improved my skills in modern styles like fades, line-ups, and beard styling, along with better client handling and service quality.

To strengthen my expertise, I completed a 6-month government-recognized diploma from IVT Institute Islamabad, focusing on advanced techniques, hygiene, and professional grooming standards.

Currently, I am working in Islamabad for the past 5 months, delivering clean, precise, and modern grooming services with a strong focus on detail, hygiene, and client satisfaction.
